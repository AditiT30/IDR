About IDR

Industry-led.
Academically grounded.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is purpose-built for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments. We close the gap between theoretical knowledge and operational readiness — developing practitioners who function from day one.

Our model bridges academic rigour and industry practice. Through a formal UK university partnership, IDR programmes are informed by cutting-edge research while being shaped entirely around what employers in financial services, critical infrastructure, and regulated technology sectors actually need.

Service Pillars

What we do.

Three integrated pillars built to develop, deploy, and sustain digital risk capability.

01 — Academy

Training & Bootcamps

Structured programmes and intensive bootcamps for students and working professionals. Academic modules, real-world case studies, and assessed live projects — building credentialled, deployable expertise in digital risk.

Bootcamps Certification CPD University Credit
02 — Incubation

Innovation & Incubation

Developing proprietary IP, next-generation risk models, and AI governance frameworks. IDR's incubation function works at the intersection of emerging technology and regulated environments — anticipating tomorrow's risk landscape.

AI Governance Risk Models R&D Proprietary IP
03 — Advisory

Advisory Services

Strategic advisory to help organisations design and mature their digital risk functions. Framework-aligned guidance across NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2, and emerging AI regulation — practical uplift, not just recommendations.
